Tech Tuesday

Macbook

Key points

The Macbook has been a major player in the computer industry, competing with PCs for decades. It undergoes regular updates by Apple to maintain its appeal and functionality.

The history

Macbooks are sold through various online retailers like BestBuy and Amazon, with prices varying based on condition, specifications, and age. Introduced in 2006, they run on Apple's macOS, offering a unique computing experience.

First generation Macbooks

The original Macbook Pro debuted in January 2006 for $1,999, featuring a webcam, Apple Remote, and iWeb software. The sleek aluminum design of the Macbook Air was introduced in 2008, setting a new standard for laptops.

What is a motherboard?

The motherboard is the largest circuit board in a computer, distributing electrical power to components like RAM, CPU, and GPU. It accommodates hardware inputs and is crucial for compatibility and system functionality.

The history

The earliest PC motherboards included basic components like the CPU and card ports. Companies like Compaq popularized these designs, leading to Intel's dominance in the motherboard market.

AI

What is artificial intelligence?

Artificial intelligence (AI) simulates human behavior and performs tasks when integrated with technology. It adapts based on experiences, making it a versatile technology capable of processing various forms of data.

Weak AI vs Strong AI

Weak AI, or Narrow AI, is task-specific, while Strong AI, or AGI (Artificial General Intelligence), aims to equal human intelligence with self-awareness and problem-solving capabilities. Research in AI continues to advance these technologies.

Blockchains

What is a blockchain?

Blockchains are secure databases that chronologically record transactions across a network. They ensure data integrity and are essential for safe, transparent transactions in various industries.

Why are blockchains important?

They eliminate the need for a central authority in transaction verification, enhancing security and reducing vulnerabilities. Common uses include financial transactions and data sharing in retail and entertainment.
